MusicMatch

Listen to songs of your choosing...

Features

Personalised playlist

Listen to music of your choosing. MusicMatch allows you add songs to your "Liked songs" list and get recommendations

Upload songs

MusicMatch allows you to upload your own songs. There are no limits to the number of songs you can upload. This feature allows users to fine tune what they listen to; whether gospel, rock, blues, reggae, or classical music, MusicMach is the app for you.

Get Recommendation

MusicMatch also allows users to get song recommendation based on certain requirements they provide. Details like Genre, Artist, and Title can be used to get song recommendation from MusicMatch.

About

This project was inspired by the curiosity to put to test the knowledge gathered during the 12 months software engineering program at ALX. The team wanted to create a simple but useful application which will be useful for music lovers around the world.
